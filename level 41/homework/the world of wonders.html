Willy Wonka is a fictional character appearing in
British author Roald Dahl's 1964 children's
novel Charlie and the Chocolate Factory,
its 1972 sequel Charlie and the Great
Glass Elevator and several films
based on those books. He is the
eccentric founder and proprietor
of the Wonka Chocolate Factory.[1]
