during the day to avoid being caught by predators. They feed primarily on seeds, fruits,
and vegetation, and will occasionally eat burrowing insects.[4] Physically, they are stout-bodied
with distinguishing features that include elongated cheek pouches extending to their shoulders, which they use to carry
